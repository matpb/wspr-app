Features

Everything you need to turn voice into text

Wspr lives in your menu bar and works with any app. Powered by WhisperKit and the Apple Neural Engine for fast, accurate, on-device transcription.

Live Transcription

Press a global hotkey from any app. Speak naturally and your words are transcribed in real time, then pasted or copied to your clipboard.

Global Hotkey

Option+Space by default, fully customizable. Start and stop recording from anywhere on your Mac without switching apps.

AI Text Rewriting

Polish transcriptions with AI. Built-in styles for minimal, professional, or concise output. Use Apple Intelligence on-device or cloud providers.

File Transcription

Drop audio or video files to transcribe them. Supports MP3, WAV, M4A, MP4, MOV and more. Ideal for meetings, lectures, and interviews.

99 Languages

Whisper models support 99 languages with automatic detection. Transcribe English, French, Spanish, Japanese, Arabic, and many more.

Menu Bar App

Always one click away. View recent transcriptions, start recording, transcribe files, and manage rewrite styles right from the menu bar.

FAQ

Common questions about Wspr

Is Wspr really free to start?

Yes. Wspr includes 50 free live transcriptions, 5 file transcriptions, and Apple Intelligence rewriting at no cost. No credit card or account required. Upgrade to Pro for $14.99 (one-time) to unlock unlimited everything.

Does my voice data stay on my Mac?

100%. All speech-to-text transcription is processed locally on your Mac using WhisperKit and the Apple Neural Engine. Your audio never leaves your device. Cloud AI is only used for optional text rewriting — and only if you choose to enable it with your own API key.

How is Wspr different from Apple's built-in dictation?

Apple's dictation has limited accuracy, no custom vocabulary, and can't rewrite your text with AI. Wspr uses OpenAI's Whisper models for superior accuracy across 99 languages, supports custom vocabulary hints, and offers AI-powered text rewriting with Apple Intelligence, OpenAI, Anthropic, or Google Gemini.

Why pay $14.99 when competitors charge $10–15 per month?

Because one month of Wispr Flow costs the same as a lifetime of Wspr. Pay once, own it forever — no recurring charges. You get on-device transcription, AI rewriting, and unlimited usage for a single payment.

What Mac do I need to run Wspr?

Wspr requires macOS 15.6 or later and an Apple Silicon Mac (M1, M2, M3, M4, or newer). The Whisper models run on the Apple Neural Engine for fast, efficient transcription.
