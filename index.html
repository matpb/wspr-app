Wspr

Voice to Text, On-Device

Wspr is a privacy-first speech-to-text app for macOS. Press a hotkey, speak, and your words appear as text — all processed locally on your Mac using the Apple Neural Engine.

  • On-device transcription powered by WhisperKit
  • AI text rewriting with Apple Intelligence, OpenAI, Anthropic, or Gemini
  • Global hotkey for instant voice-to-text from any app
  • Transcribe audio and video files
  • No accounts, no tracking, no cloud recording

Available on the Mac App Store.
