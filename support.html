Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start transcribing?

First, download a Whisper model in Settings > Whisper. Then press Option+Space (or your custom hotkey) to start recording. Press it again to stop, and your speech will be transcribed and pasted or copied to your clipboard.

Which model should I choose?

Start with Base for a good balance of speed and accuracy. If you need better accuracy for difficult audio, try Small or Medium. The Large model is the most accurate but requires more memory and processing time.

How does AI rewriting work?

After transcription, Wspr can optionally rewrite your text to fix grammar, adjust tone, or match a style. You can use Apple Intelligence (free, on-device) or cloud providers like OpenAI, Anthropic, or Gemini (requires your own API key and Wspr Pro).

Is my audio sent to the cloud?

No. All speech-to-text transcription happens entirely on your Mac. Audio never leaves your device. Cloud AI is only used for optional text rewriting (not transcription) and only if you choose to configure it.

How do I restore my purchase?

Open Wspr, go to Settings, and click "Restore Purchases" in the General tab. Your Wspr Pro purchase is tied to your Apple ID and can be restored on any Mac.

The app isn't transcribing correctly

Try a larger model (Small or Medium) for better accuracy. Make sure your microphone is working and selected correctly in System Settings. Speaking clearly and minimizing background noise also helps.
